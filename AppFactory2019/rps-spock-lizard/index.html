This is the tricky part. There are now two ways to win. So we will need to use some logic.

 

Of course, we'll still need the if...then block. But we want to check two things at once - the two ways for the player to win.

Now we are checking if the player chose 2 (paper) OR chose 4 (spock) when the computer chose rock. Paper wraps rock, player wins OR Spock vaporises rock, player wins.

 

Any other possibility, the computer wins. There are two ways for the computer to win now instead of one but we don’t have to check because those are the only two possibilities left.
