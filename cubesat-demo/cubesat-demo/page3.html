Satellites can detect wind patterns using a technology called LIDAR.


LIDAR works by shining strong ultraviolet light through the air, then looking at the reflections from dust being thrown around in the wind.


This simulation of Hurricane Sandy over the USA comes from the kind of data captured by LIDAR.


Data: National Digital Forecast Database
Simulation: hint.fm
