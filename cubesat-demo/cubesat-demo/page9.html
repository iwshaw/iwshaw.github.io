Neumayer Glacier is on the east coast of South Georgia, an island 1,200 miles east of South America.

It has retreated more than 2.5 miles (4 kilometers) during the past 16 years.

Neumayer flows into the ocean, so even a tiny change in ocean temperature can melt some of the glacier, making it smaller.
